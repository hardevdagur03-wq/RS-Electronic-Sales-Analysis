# 📊 RS Electronic Sales Analysis Dashboard

## 🚀 Project Overview

This project presents an **end-to-end Power BI dashboard** analyzing 4 years of e-commerce sales data.
The goal is to uncover **sales trends, product performance, and profitability insights** to support data-driven business decisions.

---

## 🧠 Business Objectives

* Analyze overall sales performance over time
* Identify **top and bottom performing products**
* Evaluate **profitability vs sales relationship**
* Compare performance across **two time periods**
* Understand **city-wise and promotion-based trends**

## 📊 Dashboard Pages

### 🔹 1. Overview Dashboard

![Overview](Screenshots/01_Overview.png)

**Key Insights:**

* Total Orders: **3421**
* Sales trend shows consistent growth with seasonal spikes
* **Top-performing cities:** Bhopal, Kanpur, Indore
* Promotions like **Summer Sale** drive highest discounts
* Strong positive correlation between **sales and profit**

---

### 🔹 2. Top / Bottom 5 Analysis

![Top Bottom](Screenshots/02_Top_Bottom_Analysis.png)

**Key Insights:**

* **Top Products by Sales:**

  * Apple iPhone 14
  * Apple MacBook Air
  * Sony Bravia TV

* **Bottom Products:**

  * Tupperware Lunch Box
  * L’Oreal Shampoo
  * Colgate Toothpaste

* High-selling products also dominate **profit contribution**

* Some low-selling items still generate **consistent volume**
